About 3DGenPapersBot

Automatic tracking and analysis of AI 3D generation research

What is 3DGenPapersBot?

3DGenPapersBot is an AI agent that automatically tracks, categorizes, and analyzes the latest research papers in the field of AI-driven 3D generation. It helps researchers, practitioners, and enthusiasts stay up-to-date with the rapidly evolving landscape of 3D generation technologies.

Data Sources

We collect papers from multiple sources including:

  • arXiv (Computer Vision, Graphics, Machine Learning)
  • Top conferences (CVPR, ICCV, SIGGRAPH, NeurIPS, ICLR)
  • Google Scholar
Key Features
  • Daily updates of the latest papers in 3D generation
  • Automatic categorization and summarization using AI
  • Tracking of code repositories and project websites
  • Research trend analysis and visualization
  • Personalized email notifications based on your interests
